Enfiled Pride

Building LGBTQIA+ Community in Enfield, CT.

About Us

Do you need help navigating your identity? Are you a parent who wants to support your LGBT+ child? Are you a neighbor who wants to be an ally? Enfield Pride can provide information, community, and support.

Learn More »

Get Involved

Help make Enfield a welcoming place for LGBT+ community members. Donors and volunteers support our monthly events, community outreach efforts, and mutual aid fund.

Learn More »

Upcoming Events

We celebrate diversity and inclusion all year long! Join us for a family-friendly game night, visit us at an upcoming festival, or listen in on an upcoming learning event hosted by an inspiring educator.
